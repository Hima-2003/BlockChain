About School
UNIVERSAL HIGH SCHOOL was established in 2010 and it is managed by the Pvt.Unaided.

It is located in Rural area. It is located in INKOLLU block of PRAKASAM district of ANDHRA PRADESH. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 10. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section.

English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in June. The school has a building. It has got 20 classrooms for instructional purposes.

All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has a boundary wall,play ground. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is very good and it is functional. The school has 3 boys toilet and it is functional. and 2 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a library.

The school has a computer lab for teaching and learning purposes. The school is having a home theatre which is like a digtal class room.It is having seperate hostel for boys and girls.The school is Applicable providing breakfast and milk in the morning,mid-day meal,snaks and milk in the evening. Dinner is provided for hostlers.There is bus facility for around 20 villages.Quality of education is very good..!
